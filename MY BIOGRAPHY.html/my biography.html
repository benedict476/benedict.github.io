Benedict Kisingu

May/2/1990

photography,editing and filming

Biography Benedict was born and raised in Nairobi.from an early age he exhibited a keen intrest in photography,filming and editting.benedict joined langata primary and later joined Ngoto Boys High School. Benedict joined KCA University for further studies where he learnt more about editing and filming.Benedict is a passionate editor and film maker with over a decade of experience in the industry.his expertise lies in crafting compelling narratives through seamless editting and cinematography.with a keen eye for detail and a creative approach to storytelling,Benedict has collaborated on numerous award winning projects across various genre,ranging from documentaries to feature films. His dedication to capturing moments that resonate with audience has earned him recognition for his exceptional work in the field of visual storytelling. He managed to open a company by the name BENTECH.
Functions of the company
  1. visual storytelling
  2. setting the mood and tone
  3. creating a cinematic languages
  4. enhancing action and suspense
  5. establishing time and place
